01 — About

The engineer behind the services

I'm a Backend Software Development Engineer at Accenture in Bangalore, where I design and ship distributed microservices in Java and Spring Boot — production REST APIs, event-driven systems with RabbitMQ, and the AWS infrastructure they run on.

A lot of my work is about reliability and cost: I raised uptime from 95.5% to 98.8%, cut MTTR by 60% with SLO-based alerting on Datadog and Grafana, and trimmed cloud spend by 25% through automated resource lifecycle management. I also fold security into the pipeline — Spring Security, least-privilege IAM, and SonarQube/Trivy scanning that cut critical vulnerabilities by 90%.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from KIIT (CGPA 8.6/10).

Outside work, I build backend side projects (a couple are below), keep my fundamentals sharp with 400+ DSA problems across LeetCode and GeeksforGeeks, and stay current with the distributed-systems and cloud space. I care about clean code, thoughtful system design, and software that genuinely holds up in production.
